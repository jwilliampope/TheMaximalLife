My name is Jack and living Maximally saved my life

I used to be lame. My life was stupid and silly and was I couldn't appreciate anything I had, and lamented over what I didn't have.

Things were terrible. I had a job that I hated, a home that I didn't want to live in, and I couldn't hold a relationship.

How I turned things around

I was on the verge of a breakdown when I decided to be Fabulous. I put 100% of my ratty, lame possessions in the garbage, moved into a luxurious home, and became rich and Fabulous.

It wasn't easy at all, until I decided it would be easy. Everything around me was in such a stupid and insipid place, I didn't know what else to do. And it worked.

Now I live The Fabulous Life

Now that I'm living the Fabulous Life, things are so much better. I live Maximally, I enjoy all the abundance and blessing the world has to offer.

Becoming Fabulous turned my life around, so I felt like I had no choice but to start sharing how I did it, and how others can benefit from living Maximally as well, which is why I started this site!
